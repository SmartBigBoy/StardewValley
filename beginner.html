Standard Farm has the most usable farmland, perfect for learning the planting system.

⚡ Energy ManagementTips

Good energy management iskey to efficient gaming

🍖

Energy-Restoring Food

  • Salad: Restore 35 points, sold at Bar
  • Pizza: Restore 75 points, sold at Bar
  • Cave Turnip: Foraging, restore 60 points
  • Coconut: Get from Ginger Island, restore 75 points
🧃

Permanently Increase Max

  • Delicious Fish: Probability +34 max
  • Complete Qi Quest: +1 permanent max
  • Pearl: Restore 150 +34 max
  • Shrimp Chip: Restore 35 +34 max
💡

Energy-Saving Tips

  • Drinking water is cheaper than eating food (but does not restore maximum energy)
  • Craft Spicy Eel，Always bring when going out
  • Winter sap gathering is more energy efficient than chopping trees
  • Bringing a hoe for mining saves energy

📆 FirstYearPlanning

Reasonably arrange activities by season

🌸

Spring (Year 1)

  • Plant Parsnip and Strawberry
  • Complete Museum Donation Quest
  • Unlock Mines
  • Meet All NPCs
☀️

Summer (Year 1)

  • Plant Blueberry (Key)
  • Build Sprinkler
  • Chop Trees to Obtain Wood
  • Complete The 40th Floor in the Mines
🍂

  • Plant Cranberry
  • Complete Community Center
  • Level Up Tools to Iron Level
  • Prepare Gold for Winter
❄️

Winter (Year 1)

  • Mining (Key)
  • Fishing to Earn Money
  • Social Boost Hearts
  • Prepare 2-Year Seeds

💡 BeginnerTipsTips

📍

Teleport TipTips

  • Hold Shift and click the edge of the map to teleport quickly
  • Use a feather fan to teleport to any mine entrance
  • The Guide needle in your backpack can teleport you home
🎁

Gift GivingGuide

  • Remember items that NPCs hate to avoid giving the wrong gift
  • Each NPC birthday gift gives double Hearts
  • You can check the Guide for each NPC’s liked items
⛏️

Mines Tips

  • Bring enough food and weapons
  • Elevator appears once every 5 floors
  • The Mines at 30/80/120 floors have treasure chests
💰

Save MoneyTips

  • Don’t buy chickens yet; First Year crops are more cost-effective
  • Make a sieve with branches; it’s cheaper than buying
  • Completing Community Center Quests can get you a lot of items
