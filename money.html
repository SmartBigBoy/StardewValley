💡 Core Money Strategies

Understanding the game economy is the first step to wealth

🌱

Farming is the Foundation

Growing crops is the most stable income source. Recommendations:

  • Spring: Strawberry > Parsnip
  • Summer: Blueberry > Corn
  • Fall: Cranberry > Pumpkin
🍷

Processing Increases Value

Processing raw materials into artisan goods can greatly boost profit:

  • Fruit → Wine (Value ×3)
  • Vegetable → Pickles (Value ×2)
  • Honey → Honey Barrel

💎 Other Money-Making Methods

Diversify income sources

🐷

Truffle/Pig

Truffles are highly profitable; fish like Angler Fish have very high prices. Raise pigs to collect plenty of truffles daily, easily earning over a million gold per year.

🐟

Pond Farming/h3>

Raise legendary fish or high-value fish such as Angler Fish and Crabs for solid weekly income.

💎

Mining

Iridium Ore is the most valuable mineral; paired with the Miner profession, profit doubles. Earn tens of thousands of gold stably per day.

🏆

Festival Competitions

Crop exhibition and fishing competitions, win to get gold rewards.

🦴

Archaeology/Artifacts

Collect valuable artifacts and ores to donate to the Museum for rewards and gold.

🐔

Eggs and Poultry

Raise chickens and sell eggs, or craft mayonnaise to increase value.

📅 Money-Making Strategies for Each Stage

The road to wealth accumulation from zero to millions

🌸

Year 1: Accumulation Phase

Focus on basic farming and Community Center. Plant parsnips, strawberries, and accumulate initial capital.

  • Goal：Complete Community Center
  • Income: Mainly from crop sales
☀️

Year 2: Expansion Phase

Expand farmland and build wine facilities. Start constructing the Greenhouse and planting fruit trees.

  • Goal: Build 10+ Casks
  • Income: Farming + Wine Making
🍂

Year 3: Explosion Phase

Fill the Greenhouse with Ancient Fruit Seeds for mass wine making. Raise pigs to collect truffles.

  • Goal: Daily income over 100,000g
  • Income: Wine + Truffles + Mining
