🏆 Mastery Skill

Unlock Requirement:Any two skills reach level 10

Complete the Mastery Cave quest to unlock the Mastery Skill Tree with additional bonuses and advanced recipes.

🎯
Quick leveling tips: Each skill has unique strategies. Farm harvest lots, mine with explosives, chop many trees, fish with crab pots, fight in mines.
