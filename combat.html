Combat Tips

🛡️

Keep Moving/h3>

Keep moving in combat to avoid being surrounded. Use arrow keys to circle around.

🍖

Bring Food/h3>

Always carry enough food, you can RestoreHealth in critical moments.

💍

Ring Selection/h3>

CombatRing greatly Boosts survival ability, PrioritizeObtaining it.

📍

Familiarize with Terrain/h3>

Remember the Mines layout, use terrain to trap Monsters.
