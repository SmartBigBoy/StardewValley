Achievement Overview

🏆

Achievement System

Stardew Valley has 39 achievements. Completing all achievements unlocks special rewards including exclusive content!

  • Achievements are divided into categories: Farming, Mining, Fishing, Social, Adventure, etc.
  • Completing achievements unlocks hidden content and rewards
🎯

Achievement Rewards

After completing all achievements:

  • Unlock the Achievement Showcase
  • Get the unique Green Rain Jacket
  • Unlock the hidden ending
  • Unlock "Legendary" difficulty mode
