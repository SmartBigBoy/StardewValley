️ Mines Overview

矿洞入口：位于农场北部的山区（矿工休息站附近）

开放时间：每天6:00 AM后可进入

最大深度：矿洞共有120层

🏔️

The Mines (Floors 1-80)

Elevator unlock conditions:

  • Floor 15 - Elevator unlocked
  • Floor 25 - Elevator Lv.2
  • Floor 50 - Elevator Lv.3
  • Floor 75 - Elevator Lv.4
  • Floor 80 - Bottom of normal mines
🔥

Skull Cavern (Floors 81-120)

Location: Desert area

Features:

  • Stronger monsters
  • Richer ores
  • Requires stairs or bombs
  • Can obtain Iridium ore

📍 Detailed Guide for Every 10 Floors

1-10

New Player Tutorial Area/h3>

Main ore: 石头、铜矿石(5%)

Monster: 绿虫、蝙蝠

要点：

  • Get familiar with controls and combat
  • Collect Copper Ore and Coal
  • Goal: Find the Elevator entrance at Floor 10
11-20

Copper Ore Dense Area/h3>

Main ore: 铜矿(20%)、铁矿(5%)

Monster: 绿虫、蝙蝠、Skeleton

要点：

  • Copper Ore spawns more frequently
  • Unlock Elevator at Floor 15!
  • Start collecting Coal
21-30

Day一Boss区

Main ore: 铜矿、铁矿(15%)

Monster: Skeleton、蓝虫

要点：

  • Floor 25 Boss: Slime (100HP)
  • Blue Slimes start to appear
  • Prepare your weapons
31-40

Iron Upgrade Zone/h3>

Main ore: 铁矿(25%)、金矿(5%)

Monster: Skeleton、Metal Head

要点：

  • Elevator Upgrade Tier 2 at Floor 25
  • Iron Ore is the main target
  • Craft Iron Bars to upgrade tools
41-50

Gold Ore Zone/h3>

Main ore: 金矿(15%)、铱矿(2%)

Monster: Magma Sprite、Metal Head

要点：

  • Gold Ore starts spawning at Floor 40
  • Floor 50 Boss: Skeleton King (150HP)
  • Elevator Upgrade Tier 3 at Floor 50
51-70

Advanced Mining Area/h3>

Main ore: 金矿(20%)、铱矿(8%)

Monster: Magma Sprite、Metal Head

要点：

  • Iridium Ore starts to spawn
  • Obtain Crystalarium
  • Bring enough Bombs for fast progression
71-80

Mines终点

Main ore: 金矿(25%)、铱矿(12%)

Monster: 各类高级怪物

要点：

  • Elevator Upgrade Tier 4 at Floor 75
  • Reach the bottom of The Mines at Floor 80
  • Entrance to Skull Cavern
81-120

Skeleton洞

Main ore: 铱矿(15%)、金矿(25%)

Monster: 飞蛇、黑虫、岩浆蝙蝠

要点：

  • Requires Staircases or Bombs
  • Floor 100 Boss: Mr. Qi (500HP)
  • Bring plenty of food and Bombs

Mining Tips

💣

Using Bombs

Bombs can quickly blast a large number of rocks, a key efficiency tool for mining!

获取方式：

  • Drop from defeating monsters in The Mines
  • Buy from Shop
  • Crafting Recipe (Mining Level 4)
🪜

Using Stairs

Staircases let you skip unnecessary floors and reach target depths directly.

获取方式：

  • Drop from defeating monsters in The Mines
  • Crafting Recipe
  • Buy from Desert Trader
🍖

Prepare Food

Mining consumes stamina; be sure to prepare enough healing food!

  • Health Potion - Normal Recovery
  • Chutney - Good Recovery
  • Coconut Soup - 恢复高
💡

Miner Ring

The Miner Ring allows you to glow and light up your surroundings in The Mines!

获取：博物馆捐赠15件物品

效果：照亮周围3格范围

⚠️
Skeleton洞注意事项：Skeleton洞位于沙漠，需要修复公交车或拥有船票才能进入。建议携带大量炸弹、楼梯和食物！
