Greenhouse

Ancient Fruit Wine Guide

Income Analysis (Greenhouse ×120 tiles):

  • Ancient Fruit Price: 3850 g each (after wine: 23100 g per keg)
  • Growth: 1 per tile, 28 days to mature
  • Can plant 4 cycles per year (Spring1→Summer2→Fall3→Winter4)
  • Per-tile yearly yield: 7 fruits (1 per week × 28 days)

Income Calculation:

  • Per-tile wine income: 7×23100 = 161,700 g
  • Greenhouse 120 tiles yearly income: ~19.4 million g
  • Tip: Pair with Farming Mastery (+10% sell price)
💡
Greenhouse Fruit Tree Income: Fruit trees produce once per year (coconut and orange twice a year). With Fruit Tree Mastery and Farming skill, income is very high! Plant early and wait 28 days to mature.

Automated Farm

Automation is the ultimate goal in Stardew Valley!

Sprinklers auto-water, silos auto-collect hay, shipping bins auto-sell products - freeing your hands!

💧

Sprinkler Automation

Tier 1: Basic Sprinkler (4 tiles)

Waters the 4 tiles around the center tile

Tier 2: Quality Sprinkler (8 tiles)

Waters a 3×3 area centered on the sprinkler

Tier 3: Iridium Sprinkler (24 tiles)

Waters a 5×5 area, highest efficiency

🌾

Hay Automation

Build a Silo (120 Wood + 100 Stone)

When using scythe to cut grass, hay auto-stores in silo

Silo capacity: 240 hay

Coop/Barn auto-takes hay from silo

📦

Shipping Bin

The Shipping Box will auto-sell the items in the box at the start of each day

Place multiple Shipping Boxes dispersed around the farm

Plan paths for easy harvesting and shipping

🐄

Animal Care Automation

Auto-Petter: Replaces manual petting, keeps animal mood up

Heater: Prevents mood drop in Winter

Cooler: Prevents mood drop in Summer
