Fishing Tips

🎯

Fishing条控制

When fishing, keep the green indicator bar within the fish\

技巧：

  • Observe the movement pattern of fish
  • Use浮标减少消耗
  • Big fish require more stamina patience
🎁

Fish Quality is affected by Fishing Skill Level and bobber:

  • Normal Quality — Base Selling Price
  • Silver Star — 1.5x Selling Price
  • Gold Star — 2x Selling Price

Fisher Profession grants an additional 25% boost to selling price!

📍

矿洞湖泊：有独特的地下鱼类

秘密森林：石鱼和木跃鱼的家园

沙漠：沙鱼和夜市鱼类

姜岛：独特热带鱼类

🦀

Trash Crab Cage can be placed in any water area. Check and collect yields every 8 hours.

Suggestion:

  • Use bait to increase item quantity
  • Place multiple Crab Cages to boost efficiency
  • Loot includes: Shrimp, Crab, Snail, Clam and more
