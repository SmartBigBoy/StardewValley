  • Desert Festival - Special events

⛏️ Skull Cave Guide

⚔️

Differences from Regular Mines

  • No elevator, requires exploring by yourself
  • Monsters are denser and more dangerous
  • Iridium Ore is more common
  • Has exclusive mineral nodes
  • Requires a lot of food and potions
💎

Rare Items Available

  • Iridium Ore - Most valuable mineral
  • Crystalarium - Spawns various minerals
  • Dinosaur Egg - Can be hatched
  • Meteorite Fragment - Rare Collectibles

Desert Adventure Tips

🧪

Be Prepared

Bring a lot of food, potions, and The Mines Bomb. The Skeleton Cave is larger than Normal The Mines Consume.

🎯

Choose Your Timing

Go to the Skeleton Cave on a lucky day for higher profit. Use a four-leaf clover to boost luck.

⏰

Time Management

You must leave The Desert before 22:00, otherwise you will pass out and lose Gold.

🔮

Life-Saving Equipment

Carry a Slime Ring to decrease damage from Slimes.
