🏝️ Ginger Island Guide

Home / Ginger Island

🗺️ Island Overview

Unlock conditions and island features

🔓

Unlock Conditions

  • Complete all Community Center bundles
  • Have 500g for ferry fare
  • Talk to Captain Wilford at the Beach
🏝️

Island Features

  • Tropical climate, can grow crops in winter
  • Unique Volcano Dungeon
  • Golden Walnut collection quest
  • West Island Farm area

️ Island Areas Guide

Beach Dock

Where the ferry arrives. Has a small supermarket.

  • Buy Coconuts and Starfruit Seeds/li>
  • Meet Island NPCs
  • Ferry daily

Volcano Dungeon

Challenging dungeon area with valuable mineral rewards.

  • Requires Combat Level 10
  • Defeat monsters to collect minerals
  • Rare Chests and Artifacts/li>

WestFarm

Can build cabins and plant tropical crops.

  • Build Island Hut/li>
  • Plant bananas, pineapples, etc.
  • Infinite Growth Cycle/li>

Mountain Top Observatory

View luck stats for free after unlocking.

  • Requires collecting Golden Walnuts
  • Free Lucky viewing every day
  • Special Event Trigger/li>

Golden Walnut Collection

Total 130 Golden Walnuts, distributed throughout the island

📍

Main Acquisition Methods

LocationCountAcquisition Method

Golden Walnut Milestones

  • 5 walnuts - Unlock Ferry Dock
  • 10 walnuts - Unlock Beach Resort
  • 15 walnuts - Unlock East Island Path
  • 20 walnuts - Unlock Mountain Path
  • 30 walnuts - Unlock Summit Path
  • 55 walnuts - Unlock Summit Observatory
  • 130 walnuts - Complete!

Volcano Dungeon Guide

Challenge high-difficulty dungeon, get precious rewards

⚔️

Entry Conditions

  • Automatically unlocked after unlocking Ginger Island
  • Recommended Combat level 10+
  • Prepare enough food and potions
💎

Items You Can Get

  • Iridium Ore - Most precious mineral
  • Iridium Bar - Craft top-tier equipment
  • Rare artifacts and gems
  • Golden Walnuts
