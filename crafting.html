🔥 Furnace and Ore Processing

Furnace

Basic Ore Processing equipment, used to smelt Ore into Bars.

How to unlock:Mining Lv.3 Recipe or found in Cindersap Forest

Ore × Quantity → Bar Firing Time
🪨 Copper Ore5→Copper Ingot30 Seconds
🪨 Iron Ore5→⬜ Iron Ingot60 Seconds
🪨 Gold Ore5→⭐ Gold Ingot90 Seconds
🪨 Iridium Ore5→Iridium Ingot120 Seconds
🪨 Coal1→No productUsed as fuel

⚙ ️ Production Equipment

🍯

Preserves Jar

Function:Fruit → Jelly/Wine

Product Value:

  • Jelly: Approx. 2× Selling Price
  • Wine: Approx. 3× Selling Price (including Artisan bonus)

Recipe:Wood ×25 + Stone ×10 + Coal ×1

🧪

Keg

Function:Farm Products → Processed Foods

Product Value:

  • Hops → Beer: Approx. 2×
  • Blueberry → Blueberry Wine: Approx. 3×
  • Honey → Honey Wine: Approx. 3×

Recipe:Wood ×30 + Stone ×30 + Iron Bar ×1

🧀

Cheese Press

Function:Milk → Cheese

Product Value:

  • Cow Milk (125g) → Cheese (230g)
  • Goat Milk (225g) → Goat Cheese (400g)

Recipe:Wood ×45 + Stone ×45 + Hardwood ×10

🥚

Mayonnaise Machine Mayo Machine

Function:Egg → Mayonnaise

Product Value:

  • Chicken Egg (50g) → Mayonnaise (215g)
  • Large Chicken Egg (95g) → Large Mayonnaise (385g)
  • Dinosaur Egg → Dinosaur Mayonnaise (800g)

Recipe:Wood ×15 + Stone ×15 + Hardwood ×5

🌱

Seed Maker

Function:Crops → Seeds

Workflow:

  • Place a Crop
  • 1 - 2 Days after get 2 - 3 same Seeds

Recipe:Wood ×25 + Stone ×25 + Coal ×10 + Gold Bar ×1

🐝

Bee House Bee House

Function:Produces Bee House products

Workflow:

  • Place after Bee Auto-Foraging Nectar
  • 4DaysafterHarvestHoney

Recipe:Wood ×40 + Stone ×8 + Coal ×1 + Bee House ×1

Farm Layout Suggestion

It is recommended to plan a dedicated Processing area on the Farm:

  • Place multiple small barrels together (near the Shipping Box)
  • Place Brewing barrels a bit farther away (longer Crafting time)
  • Ensure aesthetics as well as convenient Harvest
⚠️
Kegs and Preserves Jars need manual collection to continue production. Check daily!
