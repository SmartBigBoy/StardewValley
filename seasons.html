Season Overview

🌸

Spring

Days 1-28 | Suitable for short-term crops

Main Crops: Parsnip (4 Days), Potato, Cauliflower, Strawberry (Limited to Day 13)

☀️

Summer

Days 29-56 | High profit season

Main Crops: Blueberry, Melon, Tomato, Hops, Starfruit

🍂

Fall

Days 57-84 | Harvest season

Main Crops: Pumpkin, Cranberry, Corn, Sunflower

❄️

Winter

Days 85-112 | Cannot grow regular crops

Plantable: Winter Seeds | Activities: Fishing, Mining, Worm Hunting

Winter 冬季活动指南

❄️ Winter cannot grow normal crops, but there are many other activities to earn money and level up skills!

🌱

Winter Seed

Price: 0g | Can plant in winter

Obtain from wild seeds and seed makers; get Winter Roots, Crystal Fruit, etc. Low profit but boosts Foraging XP.

🪱

Fossil Hunting

Use hoe on soil after rain

Winter is the best season for digging worms. You can get artifacts, ores, seeds, etc. It is recommended to go to the Beach and Farm after rain.

❄️

Ice Pip Collection

Winter-only collectible

Forage for Winter Ice Tears near the Mines entrance every day in Winter; required important items for Community Center bundles.

Planting Tips

⏰

Growth Calculator

Each season is only 28 days! Calculate if crops will mature before season ends.

Example: Cannot plant Parsnip (4 Days) after Spring Day 22, but Strawberry (8 Days) and Fruit Trees are still plantable.

💧

Sprinkler Upgrade

Sprinklers auto-water, saving lots of time:

  • Basic Sprinkler: 4 tiles (center of 3x3)
  • Quality Sprinkler: 8 tiles (center of 5x5)
  • Iridium Sprinkler: 24 tiles (center of 7x7)
🌟

Quality Crops

Use quality fertilizer to improve crop quality (Normal/Silver/Gold):

Normal: Standard Price
Silver Star: 1.25x Price
Gold Star: 1.5x Price

🏛️

Greenhouse Farming

Unlock greenhouse after completing Community Center, grow any season crops!

Recommended Greenhouse Plants: Fruit Trees (Apricot, Peach, Orange, etc.). They take 28 Days to mature and bear fruit every year.

First-Year Survival Guide:
  • Spring: Focus on Strawberries, save as much as possible
  • Summer: Plant all Blueberries, buy Starfruit seeds if possible

  • Winter: Complete Community Center, fishing, foraging, explore mines
